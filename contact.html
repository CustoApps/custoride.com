Frequently Ask Question?

What is CustoRide?

CustoRide is a full-stack TMS (Transportation Management Software) that helps you plan transport for both people and cargo alike. From booking a ride, to tracking a driver's performance over time.

Who uses CustoRide?

CustoRide is for those who need to manage logistics. Think Taxi fleets, Paratransit, Last-mile delivery, Campus shuttles, Ride-share networks

Can CustoRide be self-hosted?

CustoRide can be self-hosted as well as used through our SaaS product. Please send us your requirements and a Solutions Engineer with get back to you with the next steps

What are CustoRide's open source plans?

We're planning to release a fully-compatible open source version of CustoRide Core (TMS) when there is enough interest in the community to learn and self-host CustoRide. Fill out a contact form if interested to be added to our beta list!

What are CustoRide's limitations?

CustoRide is software, and will not necessarily do everything for you. We provide a customizable interface for business leaders to configure the best experience they need. Reach out with your requirements and our Solutions experts will guide you with recommendations

Can CustoRide interface with other systems?

Absolutely. CustoRide TMS was built with a plug-in architecture. Let us know which systems you need to connect to, and we will either suggest an existing solution, or work with you to build an adapter
